# DeepResearch

**Multi-agent AI research reports. 80% of McKinsey quality at 5% of the cost.**

## The Problem

Professional research reports cost $10-50K and take weeks. Single-agent AI research (one ChatGPT conversation) produces shallow, biased results. Neither option works for most businesses.

## The Solution

DeepResearch uses 4-6 specialized AI agents researching simultaneously -- web, academic, competitor, fact-checker -- producing cross-validated reports in 30 minutes.

## Features

- Multi-agent parallel research (4-6 specialists)
- Cross-validation and fact-checking built in
- Source diversity (web, academic, competitive)
- Confidence scores on all claims
- Actionable recommendations with evidence
